Leave My Temple VR

Tango Byte • 2024 • Unity

Defend your temple, become a god.

Adopting various god-like abilities, the player must fight against different mythological enemies charging towards your last standing temple. You will be assisted by human defenses that can be built with the help of mystical coins. As the player, dare to discover the unique interactions you can create as a god
