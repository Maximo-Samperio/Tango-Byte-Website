About Tango Byte

Crafting immersive gaming experiences since 2022 in Buenos Aires, Argentina

Our Journey

Tango Byte was founded in the heart of Buenos Aires in 2022 by a group of passionate developers, artists, and designers. United by our love for storytelling and innovation, we've been dedicated to building emotionally resonant games that push creative boundaries.

  • Location: Buenos Aires, Argentina
  • Founded: 2022
  • Passion: Psychological horror, puzzle, and VR experiences
